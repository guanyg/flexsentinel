Our Vision

We want practical, easy-to-deploy monitoring that gives maintenance teams timely, interpretable signals about structural health so they can plan work, avoid failures, and reduce downtime.

Detect early

Flag non-obvious trends before they become failures.

Use Cases

Class 8 frame & axle monitoring

Track fatigue accumulation on tractors and trailers across routes. Monitor structural stress patterns in real-time to predict maintenance needs before critical failures occur.

Post-event checks

Quick structural checks after impacts, rollovers, or overload events. Get immediate assessments of structural integrity to make informed decisions about vehicle safety and repair priorities.

RV fleet safety

Detect chassis and suspension wear for long-haul vehicles. Monitor structural health over extended journeys to ensure passenger safety and reduce unexpected breakdowns.
